The Early Life


“No Half Measures” by Mike Ehrmantraut

Bailey Dennis was born on September 30th, 1997, in Lynchburg, Virginia. Growing up, Bailey didn't consider his upbringing the best, having to move a bunch and change schools. This realistically caused him to have to abandon some friendships he had made at the schools he had been to and consistently make new friends every time he moved into a new school setting. His family split while growing up, but he has a mother named Tonya, a father named Greg, and a sister named Mackenzie.




The Adult Life


After high school, Bailey would pursue college off and on until he finished his degree at Liberty University in 2024. After graduation, Bailey found the love of his life, Olivia. They both eventually got married on May 6th of 2023, bought a house, and are now their own family with their son, Beckham, all within the span of 2 years. He currently works as a paraplanner at a retirement firm. For those who don't know, a paraplanner is a technical financial professional who supports financial advisors by conducting research, analyzing financial data, and drafting tailored financial plans and reports. This job lines up with Bailey at his core, as he considers himself a realist and has a logic-based mindset who seeks to be kind to others.
